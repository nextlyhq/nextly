The collections list in the builder now uses the unified admin data table. Clicking a row opens the collection in the builder, actions live in a consistent menu, and the list collapses to cards on narrow screens. Locked code-first collections stay read-only: their selection checkbox is disabled, the row does not open on click, and they are excluded from bulk delete. Search, source and status filters, column visibility, and pagination are unchanged.
