Make the form builder's two-pane layout responsive. The canvas and the field/properties panel stay side by side on desktop and stack vertically below the admin's `lg` breakpoint, so on tablet and mobile the fixed-width panel no longer overflows or overlaps the canvas. When stacked, the panel drops below the canvas and its divider moves to the top edge.
